## Versioning Scheme

CMS versioning generally follows Laravel's major (not paradigm) releases. 
For Laravel `5.6`, for instance, the CMS Core version is `1.6.*`.

This translates into this versioning scheme: `<major>.<laravel-major>.<minor>`.
